I'm Charlie, a full-stack web and iOS developer/designer. I graduated in 2026 with an HBSc in computer science, mathematics, and forensics.

Since I was a kid building games in Scratch, I knew coding was what I wanted to do with my life. My first app was a highschool side project, a free music streaming platform called Openmusic. The goal was P2P distribution in a user-friendly, high quality app. I succeeded, and now serve 3500+ users.
Since then I've been obsessed with building apps that feel good to use. I focus on the small UI/UX details that turn something functional into something fluid, usable, and genuinely enjoyable.
